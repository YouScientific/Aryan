1. What is Science Stream?

After class 10th, if you choose Science, you study subjects like:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Math or Biology
  • English

Science stream helps you become an engineer, doctor, scientist, and more!

2. Career Options After Class 12th

With PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Math):

  • Engineering (Computer, Civil, Mechanical, etc.)
  • Architecture (B.Arch)
  • Defense (Army, Navy – NDA Exam)
  • Pilot (With flying school and tests)

With PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology):

  • MBBS (Doctor)
  • BDS (Dentist)
  • Nursing
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

With PCMB (All Four Subjects):

You can choose from both PCM and PCB careers!

3. Entrance Exams

  • JEE – For Engineering
  • NEET – For Medical Courses
  • NDA – For Defense Services
  • NATA – For Architecture

4. Other Science Careers

  • Scientist (with B.Sc. and M.Sc.)
  • Biotechnologist
  • Environmental Scientist
  • Computer Programmer (with BCA)
